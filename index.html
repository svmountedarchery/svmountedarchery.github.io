Grounded Practice

We attend the practices of the Bay Area Asian Archers, which are free to attend and open to everyone. They rotate between local public archery ranges including: Kings Mountain Archers in Huddart County Park in Woodside; Bowhunters Unlimited in Stevens Creek Country Park near Cupertino; Redwood Bowman behind the Chabot Space and Science Center in Oakland; and SF Archers in Pacifica (there's a $5/day fee for this range). Check out the group's Google Calendar to see when and where we're meeting next. You can also connect with us by way of the Bay Area Mounted Archery Meetup Group!

Mounted Practice

As for horseback riding, we all have individual arrangements. Some of us own horses, some lease, and we all take lessons. Our group is currently looking for a place in the Peninsula or South Bay area where we can all practice together. While we hope you'll join us in the search, you should also know about the California Centaurs, a Mounted Archery Association of the Americas (MA3) affiliated club based in Petaluma that gives instruction in horseback archery and holds regular practices and clinics.
