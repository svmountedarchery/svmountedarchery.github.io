We are a group of horseback archers based in the San Francisco Bay Area. We practice, compete, give demonstrations, trade stories, and have fun together.

Grounded Practice

We practice with the Bay Area Asian Archers, which are free to attend and open to everyone. They rotate between local public archery ranges including: Kings Mountain Archers in Huddart County Park in Woodside; Bowhunters Unlimited in Stevens Creek Country Park near Cupertino; Redwood Bowman behind the Chabot Space and Science Center in Oakland; and SF Archers in Pacifica (there's a $5/day fee for this range). Check out the group's Google Calendar to see when and where they're meeting next. Beginners are welcome.

Mounted Practice

As for horseback riding, we all have individual arrangements. Some of us own a horse, some lease, and some take lessons.

We participate in IHAA and MA3 postal matches and rankings as members of the California Centaurs, a club based in Petaluma that gives instruction in horseback archery. The pictures of us on horseback on this page were taken there.
